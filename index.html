The Reviews

Diablo is a classic dark genre game where you control your hero in the third-person perspective. You can team up with friends from the internetz to take down Diablo himself in this action packed, quick paced game!

Hearthstone is a light hearted approach to a classic game like MTG. This is a 1 player game where you construct a deck of cards and take on a live opponent through the internetz. This game will make you think like a chess player as you discover the subtle layers of strategy!

Starcraft is the game to play if you like RTS style games. If you like performing 600 actions per minute then this is your game. This game will really make you feel horrible at video games when you try to take it online and show your skill.
